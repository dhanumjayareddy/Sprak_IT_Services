# Product Requirement Document (PRD)

# Spark IT Services — Corporate Website


## 1. Product Overview

**Product Name:** Spark IT Services Website
**Type:** Corporate Marketing Website
**Goal:** Generate **training enrollments, placement requests, and corporate hiring partnerships**.

The website must communicate that **Spark IT Services is a premium talent development and staffing partner**, not just a training institute.

It should highlight:

* IT training programs
* Non-IT job training
* Placement assistance
* Corporate hiring partnerships
* Interview preparation & scheduling
* Success stories

The site should visually resemble **modern SaaS / consulting websites** (Stripe, Deel, Cognizant-style aesthetics).
---
# 2. Target Users

### Candidate Segment

Fresh graduates or job switchers looking for:

* SAP training
* Java development
* Software testing
* Medical coding
* Billing
* Chat process jobs

Goals:

* Get trained
* Crack interviews
* Get placed

---

### Corporate Clients

Companies needing:

* Skilled candidates
* Pre-screened developers
* Contract talent
* Bulk hiring support

Goals:

* Reduce hiring cost
* Reduce training time
* Access trained talent pipeline
